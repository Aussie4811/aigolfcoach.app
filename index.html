Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

AI Golf Coach ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. By using AI Golf Coach, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Who We Are

AI Golf Coach is a Canadian golf coaching application powered by Ace, your personal AI Golf Coach. We are based in Canada and operate under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA). For privacy inquiries, contact us at: aigolfcoach@proton.me

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account we collect your name, email address, and password (encrypted). You may optionally provide your age range, dominant hand, handicap, experience level, typical score, play frequency, and golf goals.

Golf Performance Data

We collect golf data you enter including round scores, course names, fairways hit, greens in regulation, putts, birdies, pars, bogeys, and other scoring statistics. We also collect launch monitor data including club speed, ball speed, launch angle, spin rate, carry distance, and related metrics.

Swing Videos and Photos

With your permission, we collect swing videos and photos you record or upload for analysis by Ace. These files are stored securely in Cloudflare R2 cloud storage and accessible only to you.

GPS Round Tracking Data

When you use the GPS Round Tracker, we collect your precise GPS location approximately every 5 seconds throughout your round. GPS data is stored locally during the round, uploaded when you save, used only to generate your round replay map, never shared with third parties, and deleted when you delete the round or your account.

Social and Community Data

When you use The Clubhouse social features, we collect posts, comments, likes, follow relationships, and profile information you choose to make public.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalised AI golf coaching through Ace
  • Analyse your golf performance and generate insights
  • Generate your round replay map from GPS tracking data
  • Save and retrieve your golf stats, sessions, and history
  • Power social features including The Clubhouse community feed
  • Improve our AI coaching algorithms and app features
  • Send app notifications (if you enable them)
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Background Location Collection

AI Golf Coach collects your location in the background ONLY when you have actively started a GPS round tracking session. Background location collection only occurs during an active tracked round, automatically stops when you end the round, and can be stopped at any time by ending the round or revoking location permissions. We never collect background location outside of active round tracking sessions.

5. AI Coaching and Third Party Services

Our AI coaching feature (Ace) is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you request an AI analysis, relevant golf data and your profile information is sent to Anthropic's servers to generate coaching responses. No GPS or location data is ever sent to Anthropic. Your data is stored on Supabase with industry-standard encryption. Swing videos are stored in Cloudflare R2.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not display advertising and do not share data with advertisers. We may share your information only with service providers (Supabase, Anthropic, Cloudflare) who assist in app operations, if required by law, or with your explicit consent.

7. Your Rights

Under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA) you have the right to access, correct, delete, and export your personal data, and to withdraw consent for data processing. California residents (CCPA) and EU/UK residents (GDPR) have additional rights. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at aigolfcoach@proton.me

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL), encrypted password storage, secure cloud storage, and row-level security policies on all database tables ensuring users can only access their own data.

9. Children's Privacy

AI Golf Coach is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions: aigolfcoach@proton.me · aigolfcoach.app
