Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

AI Golf Coach ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. By using AI Golf Coach, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Who We Are

AI Golf Coach is a Canadian golf coaching application powered by Ace, your personal AI Golf Coach. We are based in Canada and operate under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA). For privacy inquiries, contact us at: aigolfcoach@proton.me

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account we collect your name, email address, and password (encrypted). You may optionally provide your age range, dominant hand, handicap, experience level, typical score, play frequency, and golf goals. You may sign in using Apple Sign In or Google Sign In, in which case we receive basic profile information from those providers.

Golf Performance Data

We collect golf data you enter including round scores, course names, fairways hit, greens in regulation, putts, birdies, pars, bogeys, and other scoring statistics. We also collect launch monitor data including club speed, ball speed, launch angle, spin rate, carry distance, and related metrics. Shot tracking and dispersion data is also collected when you use the shot tracker feature.

Swing Videos and Photos

With your permission, we collect swing videos and photos you record or upload for analysis by Ace. These files are stored securely in Cloudflare R2 cloud storage. Videos are associated with your account and accessible only to you.

GPS Round Tracking Data

When you use the GPS Round Tracker feature, we collect your precise GPS location approximately every 5 seconds throughout your round. This includes latitude, longitude, timestamp, and accuracy data. Location tracking continues in the background when the app is minimized during an active round. GPS data is:

  • Stored locally on your device during the round
  • Uploaded to our servers when you save the round
  • Used to generate your round replay map and calculate distances
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Deleted when you delete the round or your account

You can revoke location permissions at any time in your device settings, which will disable the GPS round tracker feature. Location data is not collected when a round is not actively being tracked.

Practice and Fitness Data

We collect practice session data including drill history, completed exercises, fitness screening results, workout session duration, and mental game session history.

Social and Community Data

When you use The Clubhouse social features, we collect posts you create, comments, likes, follow relationships, and profile information you choose to make public. Public posts are visible to all registered users. Private profile content is only visible to approved followers.

Device Information

We automatically collect device type, operating system, app version, and usage analytics to improve app performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalised AI golf coaching through Ace
  • Analyse your golf performance and generate insights
  • Generate your round replay map from GPS tracking data
  • Save and retrieve your golf stats, sessions, and history
  • Power social features including The Clubhouse community feed
  • Improve our AI coaching algorithms and app features
  • Send app notifications (if you enable them)
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Background Location Collection

AI Golf Coach collects your location in the background ONLY when you have actively started a GPS round tracking session. Background location collection:

  • Only occurs during an active tracked round
  • Automatically stops when you end the round
  • Is clearly indicated in the app with an active round indicator
  • Uses approximately 8–12% battery over a 4-hour round
  • Can be stopped at any time by ending the round or revoking location permissions

We never collect background location outside of active round tracking sessions. iOS users will see a blue status bar indicator when background location is active. We request "Always" location permission solely to support uninterrupted round tracking when the app is backgrounded during a round.

5. AI Coaching and Third Party Services

Anthropic Claude API

Our AI coaching feature (Ace) is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you request an AI analysis, relevant golf data and your profile information is sent to Anthropic's servers to generate coaching responses. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this data processing. We do not send personally identifying information beyond what is needed for coaching context. No GPS or location data is ever sent to Anthropic.

Supabase

We use Supabase to store your account data, golf statistics, social posts, and app data. Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption and row-level security policies that ensure only you can access your own data.

Cloudflare R2

Swing videos and photos you upload are stored in Cloudflare R2 cloud storage. Files are stored with access controls tied to your account. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to this storage service.

Golf Course API

Our GPS caddie feature uses a third-party Golf Course API to retrieve course layout information. No personal data is shared with this service.

Apple Sign In / Google Sign In

If you use Sign In with Apple or Google Sign In, those providers share basic profile information with us (name and email). Their respective privacy policies govern the data they collect from you.

6. Camera, Microphone and Photo Library

AI Golf Coach requests access to your device camera, microphone, and photo library solely for the purpose of recording and uploading swing videos and photos for analysis by Ace. We will never:

  • Access your camera or microphone without your knowledge
  • Share your videos or photos with third parties beyond secure cloud storage
  • Use your media for any purpose other than golf coaching

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not display advertising and do not share data with advertisers. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers (Supabase, Anthropic, Cloudflare) who assist in app operations under strict data processing agreements
  • If required by law or valid legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users
  • With your explicit consent

Social content you post publicly in The Clubhouse is visible to other registered users of AI Golf Coach. This is not considered sharing with third parties as it is a feature you actively choose to use.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

  • Swing videos and photos are deleted from Cloudflare R2 immediately upon your request or account deletion
  • GPS round tracking data is deleted when you delete the associated round or your account
  • Social posts you delete are removed immediately
  • AI analysis history is deleted with your account

9. Your Rights

Under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA) and applicable international regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Revoke location permissions at any time

California residents (CCPA): You have additional rights including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

EU/UK residents (GDPR): You have additional rights including the right to restrict processing and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: aigolfcoach@proton.me

10. Children's Privacy

AI Golf Coach is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete that information.

11. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Encrypted password storage
  • Secure cloud storage via Supabase and Cloudflare R2
  • Row-level security policies on all database tables ensuring users can only access their own data
  • Server-side rate limiting on AI features
  • Regular security reviews

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in servers located outside Canada, including in the United States, where our service providers (Supabase, Anthropic, Cloudflare) operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this policy and, where appropriate, sending you a notification through the app. Your continued use of AI Golf Coach after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns:
